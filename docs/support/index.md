# Support The Project

Keep the labs free, clean, and useful for students who are learning DevOps at
home.

This project should not become an ad wall. The public lab guides, learning
paths, safety runbooks, and project catalog stay open. Support is for people and
organizations who want the portal maintained, reviewed, and expanded without
interrupting the learning experience.

## Sponsor Principles

- No display ads.
- No popups.
- No tracking-heavy funnels.
- No paywall on the core lab guides.
- No sponsored ranking of projects.
- No credential sharing or managed access to student cloud accounts.
- Sponsor mentions should be quiet, relevant, and clearly labeled.

## Sponsor Brief

Sponsors should support the mission, not control the curriculum.

Good sponsorship looks like:

- Helping keep the project free for learners.
- Funding updates for cloud, Kubernetes, CI/CD, DevSecOps, and observability labs.
- Supporting documentation quality, accessibility, and cleanup guidance.
- Being listed on a sponsor page or brief mention, not inserted into every lab.

Bad sponsorship looks like:

- Ads inside the learning flow.
- Tool lock-in disguised as curriculum.
- Requiring students to create accounts before they understand the lab.
- Asking for access to student credentials or cloud accounts.
